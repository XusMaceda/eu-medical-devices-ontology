Introduction back to ToC

This ontology has been developed as part of PEC 2 for the course Knowledge Representation. Its purpose is to model the European medical devices ecosystem using Semantic Web technologies and OWL. The ontology represents medical devices, manufacturers, regulatory frameworks, device classifications, known risks, incidents, affected patients and field safety corrective actions.

The model has been populated from structured CSV datasets and enriched with reasoning rules expressed in SWRL, allowing the inference of additional knowledge such as high-risk devices, corrected incidents and manufacturers involved in reported incidents.

The ontology also reuses external vocabularies and RDF resources in order to improve interoperability and semantic integration.

EU Medical Devices Ontology: Description back to ToC

An OWL ontology for representing medical devices marketed in the European Union, including manufacturers, device types, regulatory frameworks, risk classes, known risks, incidents, affected patients and field safety corrective actions. The ontology is populated from CSV datasets and supports semantic reasoning through SWRL rules and external RDF links.

Infer corrected incidentsback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Classifies incidents with an associated field safety corrective action as corrected incidents.

Incident c(?i) , has corrective action op(?i,?a) -> Corrected incident c(?i)

Infer high-risk devicesback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Device c(?d) , has risk class op(?d,?ClassIII) -> High-risk device c(?d)
